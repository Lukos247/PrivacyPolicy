Loan Terms Disclosure

The Application "Onlayn qarzlar" fully complies with the Google Play Personal Loans policy. The minimum repayment period for any loan offer displayed in the Application is 61 (sixty-one) days from the date the loan is issued. The Application does NOT promote, advertise, market or facilitate any personal loans that require repayment in full in 60 days or less.

Standard Loan Terms Available to Every User

  • Minimum repayment period: 61 days. Maximum repayment period: 365 days. For large credit products (auto loans, mortgages) — up to 5 years.
  • First online loan — at a 0% rate, when repaid on time (introductory offer).
  • Annual Percentage Rate (APR) range: from 0% to 36% per year.
  • Credit rate: from 0% to 31% per year.
  • Transparent terms: no hidden commissions or additional fees beyond those disclosed in the loan agreement.

Representative Example

Principal: UZS 25,000,000
Term: 12 months
Rate: 9% per annum
Total repayment amount: UZS 26,218,780
Of which interest: UZS 1,218,780

Penalties and Late Payments

  • Late payment penalty: 0.1% of the loan amount per day, capped at a maximum of 10% of the total loan amount.
  • In case of prolonged delay, the borrower's information is transferred to the Credit Information Bureau of the Republic of Uzbekistan in accordance with applicable law.
  • Term extension is available upon timely request and payment of the interest accrued for the current period.

Legal Information

The Total Cost of Credit is calculated in accordance with the requirements of:

  • The Law of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On Consumer Credit" No. ZRU-33 dated 6 May 2006;
  • The Law of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On Non-Bank Credit Organizations and Microfinance Activities" No. ZRU-765 dated 20 April 2022.

The Total Cost of Credit includes the principal amount, interest and other payments stipulated in the loan agreement. Microfinance activities in the Republic of Uzbekistan are regulated by the Central Bank of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

Total Cost of Credit (TCC) range: from 9.9% to 25.227%.

Aggregator Status

The "Onlayn qarzlar" application operates as an aggregator of financial product offers and does not engage in lending activities directly. The Operator (AR Reklaam OU) is not a lender, does not issue loans, and does not enter into loan agreements with Users. No separate financial license is required for this type of activity.

The Application provides Users with access to offers from banks and microfinance organizations that are licensed and regulated under the legislation of the Republic of Uzbekistan. All loan agreements are concluded directly between the User and the chosen licensed Partner, on the Partner's own platform and under the Partner's own terms.

Service Features

  • Interest-free first loan for new users (introductory promotion).
  • Automated application review — the system operates 24 hours a day.
  • Fast transfer — funds are credited to the User's bank card within the shortest possible time after approval by the Partner.
  • Convenient comparison — dozens of offers from licensed Partners in a single application.
  • User data is kept strictly confidential and stored securely (see the Privacy Policy below).

Eligibility

The Application and the loan offers within it are available exclusively to citizens of the Republic of Uzbekistan who are 18 years of age or older and possess a valid national ID document and an active bank card issued in the Republic of Uzbekistan.
